# JARVIS – AI-Powered Contextual Business Assistant 🚀

**JARVIS** is an AI-powered contextual business memory and decision-support platform designed for freelancers, consultants, and professionals. Unlike traditional chatbots, JARVIS maintains long-term business context by storing project discussions, client requirements, pricing negotiations, and historical decisions, enabling users to make informed decisions based on past experiences.

The platform leverages **Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG)** and **Large Language Models (LLMs)** to retrieve relevant information from historical conversations and generate intelligent recommendations, risk assessments, and actionable insights.

---

## ✨ Features

### 🧠 Contextual Business Memory

* Stores complete project conversations and business interactions.
* Maintains long-term context across multiple projects and clients.
* Tracks requirements, discussions, and decision history.

### 🔍 Semantic Search & Retrieval

* Converts conversations into vector embeddings.
* Retrieves relevant past experiences using semantic similarity search.
* Provides context-aware responses instead of keyword-based matching.

### 🤖 AI-Powered Decision Support

* Generates recommendations based on historical data.
* Identifies potential risks and opportunities.
* Assists in project planning and client management.

### 🎤 Voice & Chat Interaction

* Supports voice-based communication.
* Speech-to-text and text-to-speech capabilities.
* Natural conversational interface.

### 🔐 Secure Access

* JWT-based authentication and authorization.
* Secure storage and retrieval of business information.
